Asthma

Asthma is a chronic illness that affects the lungs, which causes constriction of airways making it harder to breathe. Constriction of the airway muscles is due to the sensitivity resulting in flare-up producing extra mucus. Asthma flare-up may begin slowly or aggravate quickly based on exposure to various factors like weather, pollen, allergens, etc. In Australia, one in nine people are affected by asthma. Asthma in children is one of the most common reasons for children missing school and visiting doctors in Australia.
